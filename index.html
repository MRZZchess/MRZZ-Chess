MRZZ Chess

🌐 1. The Expanded Battlefield (12×8 Grid)

MRZZ Chess expands the horizons of regular chess into a massive 12 rows by 8 columns setup. This creates two distinct zones: the traditional starting ranks and an intense "Boxing Arena" in the middle, expanding operational depth and long-range tactical planning.

⚔️ 2. The Sword (⚔️ / 🗡️) - Proximity Weapon

The Sword acts as a directional line-of-sight weapon. It has two distinct modes controlled by the "Flip Sword 🔄" action:

  • Forward Attack Mode: Moves/captures in a straight vertical line forward, or in wide diagonals backward (acting like a triangular spear).
  • Backward Attack Mode (Flipped): Reverses its trajectory entirely to strike straight backward or slash forward diagonally.
  • Crucial Rule: Executing a "Flip Sword" counts as a full move, passing the turn immediately to the opponent and modifying the active clock.

✨ 3. Bounced Bishop (✦ Star Badge)

An engineering marvel on the board: retains its standard diagonal moves, but if it hits the left or right boundaries of the grid, it bounces off the walls dynamically like a laser light, continuing its trajectory across the board to trap unsuspecting opponents.

🐎 4. Golden Knight (✦ Star Badge)

This massive cavalry unit merges two movement profiles: it can jump in the traditional L-shape pattern (2 squares out, 1 square sideways) AND it possesses the defensive ability to step 1 square in any direction (like a King), making it a lethal close-quarters piece.

👑 5. Amazon Variants (👑 Crown Badge)

  • Standard Amazon: Fuses the destructive linear powers of a Queen with the jumping agility of a Knight.
  • Bounced Amazon (✦ Badge): The ultimate piece. Fuses the Knight jumps with a Bounced Queen mechanism, meaning its diagonal vectors can bounce off the side walls infinitely until blocked.

♟️ 6. Adaptive Boxing Pawns & Ultimate Promotion

Pawns change behavior based on their exact grid coordinates (Vertical Rank):

  • Ranks 1-3 & 10-12 (Base Camps): Extremely flexible. Pawns can step or capture 1 square in any direction (Omnidirectional movement).
  • Ranks 4-5 & 8-9 (Mid-fields): Normal pawn rules apply—1 step forward, capture diagonally forward.
  • Ranks 6-7 (The Boxing Ring): Intense close-combat. Can move 1 step straight forward or slide 1 step left/right horizontally into open files to block paths.
  • 👑 Ultimate Promotion Rank: Upon reaching the furthest final rank of the opponent's side, the pawn immediately gains an Ultimate Promotion. The player can transform it into a devastating Queen, Rook, Bishop, or Knight.

⏳ 7. Real-Time Watch & Competitive Undo

  • Continuous Navigation Clock: Reviewing the match using Back or Forward timeline controls will never freeze or disrupt the ticking game clock. The match remains actively competitive.
  • Anti-Time-Cheating Undo (Offline): When a player presses Undo, their clock is not rewarded or rolled back—their spent time remains lost. Only the opponent’s clock is restored to exactly what it was before that move was made.

🤝 8. Standard Chess Draw Regulations

The battlefield fully respects traditional chess guidelines. A game can end in a draw under the following conditions:

  • Stalemate (التعادل المخنوق): Triggered automatically if the active player has no legal moves left while their King is NOT in check.
  • Mutual Agreement (الاتفاق المتبادل): Both commanders agree to end the dispute in a truce using the "Offer Draw 🤝" feature.

📡 9. Forfeit & Disconnection Safeguard

  • Unstoppable Clocks: Under no circumstances will the live match clock freeze if a player leaves the tab or disconnects.
  • 60-Second Forfeit Rule: If a player disconnects or exits the battle arena page, a **60-second grace countdown** starts in the background. If they do not return to the lobby within 60 seconds, they are instantly declared **Resigned (منسحب)**, awarding victory to the remaining player.
