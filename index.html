📜 Game Manual & Special Rules:

Welcome to MRZZ Chess Pro, a battlefield expanded to a massive 12×8 grid. Master the classic pieces along with your newly engineered mythical arsenal:

  • The Sword (⚔️/🗡️): A deadly direction-locked projectile unit. Alternates angles via the "Flip Sword 🔄" button.
  • Bounced Bishop (✦ Badge): Advanced tactical unit moving diagonally with unique **left and right wall bouncing** abilities. Identified by a gold badge.
  • Giant Knight (✦ Badge): Cavalry piece combining Knight leaps with 1-step multi-directional King movements.
  • Amazon Variants (👑 Wearing Crown): Powerhouses. Regular Amazon blends Queen/Knight. Bounced Amazon adds custom diagonal wall-bouncing physics.
  • Dynamic Timeline Control: Navigate history fluidly with comprehensive rewind tools under the main operational theatre.
