📜 Game Manual & Special Rules:

Welcome to MRZZ Chess, a battlefield expanded to a massive 12×8 grid. Master the classic pieces along with your newly engineered mythical arsenal:

  • The Sword (⚔️/🗡️): A deadly direction-locked projectile unit. It attacks continuously along its designated angles. You can alternate its attacking orientation at the cost of your turn using the "Flip Sword 🔄" button.
  • Bounced Bishop (🎔/🪖 Special): An advanced tactical unit that moves diagonally but holds the unique ability to **bounce off the left and right walls** of the battlefield to surprise enemies.
  • Golden Knight (♘/♞ Special): A massive cavalry piece that combines the standard L-shaped leap of a Knight with the 1-step multi-directional movement of a King.
  • Amazon & Bounced Amazon (♕/♛ Special): Ultimate powerhouse units. The regular Amazon combines Queen and Knight movements. The Bounced Amazon combines Knight leaps with the wall-bouncing diagonal physics of the Bounced Bishop.
  • Pawn & Board Zones: Pawns have unique dynamic mechanics depending on their row height. Reaching the farthest rows (Row 12 for White, Row 1 for Black) triggers the Ultimate Promotion prompt.
  • How to Move: Simply Drag & Drop any piece, or click the piece first to highlight all legal/capture moves, then click the destination square.
